# Competitive Analysis

## Market Category
Influencer marketing platforms / Creator economy tools

## Landscape Overview

### Category 1 — Brand-Influencer Matching Platforms
Focus: Discovery and outreach only
Gap: No end-to-end campaign tracking,
     no attribution, no automated payouts

### Category 2 — Affiliate Networks
Focus: Link tracking and commission management
Gap: No creator discovery layer,
     no creator-side monetization tools

### Category 3 — Creator Monetization Tools
Focus: Courses, subscriptions, community
Gap: No brand campaign layer,
     no affiliate tracking or discovery

### Category 4 — Social Media Native Tools
Focus: Boosted posts, paid partnerships
Gap: Platform-locked, no cross-platform tracking,
     no creator storefront or community ownership

## The Gap We Targeted
No single platform combined all four:
- Performance-based campaign management with attribution
- AI-driven creator discovery
- Creator-side monetization (storefront, courses, community)
- End-to-end automated payouts with fraud protection

## Our Differentiation
| Feature | Competitors | Our Platform |
|---|---|---|
| Campaign pricing models | Flat fee mostly | CPC, CPV, CPP, CPCall |
| Attribution | Basic or none | UTM per campaign per creator |
| Creator storefront | Rare | Built-in with templates |
| Community monetization | Separate tools | Integrated |
| Course/subscription | Separate tools | Built-in |
| Fraud detection | Rare | Built into payout logic |
| Payout automation | Manual mostly | Fully automated with hold |
| AI creator matching | Basic filters | ML-based recommendations |

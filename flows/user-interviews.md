# User Research — Themes

> Raw transcripts not included.
> Summarized themes from discovery interviews
> conducted with brands and creators pre-build.

## Brand-Side Themes

### No accountability on influencer spend
Brands were running campaigns on trust alone.
No way to verify if a post drove actual purchases.
Attribution was self-reported by creators in many cases.

### Fraud was a known and accepted problem
Multiple brands had paid for fake engagement.
No tooling to detect it. Treated as a cost of doing business.
A fraud detection layer was immediately valued in testing.

### Operational overhead was the biggest pain
Managing 10+ creators meant 10+ DM threads,
10+ payment transfers, 10+ performance check-ins.
Brands wanted one dashboard, not a coordination job.

### ROI visibility was the unlock
Brands weren't against influencer marketing —
they were against spending without knowing if it worked.
Performance-based models with real attribution
were the primary reason brands would switch platforms.

## Creator-Side Themes

### Income unpredictability was the core anxiety
Most creators had experienced months with no brand deals.
One-off campaigns meant feast-or-famine income cycles.
Recurring revenue streams (subscriptions, courses)
were a strong pull factor.

### Payout trust was broken
Late payments, missed commissions, and no transparency
were common complaints across creators interviewed.
A clear payout timeline with visible pending earnings
was the most requested feature before any campaign tooling.

### Creators wanted to own their audience
Dependency on social platforms felt fragile.
A storefront and community they controlled —
not owned by Instagram or YouTube —
was a meaningful value proposition.

### Managing deals was a part-time job
Email threads, spreadsheet tracking, manual invoicing.
Creators wanted one place to see all active campaigns,
earnings, and link performance.

---

## Key Insights That Shaped the Product

| Insight | Product Response |
|---|---|
| Brands won't pay without attribution | UTM-based tracking per link per campaign |
| Fraud is a trust killer | Anomaly detection + hold period before payout |
| Creators need recurring revenue | Storefront, courses, community in v1 |
| Payout trust is table stakes | Real-time pending earnings + clear hold timeline |
| Operational overhead kills scale | Unified dashboard for brands and creators |

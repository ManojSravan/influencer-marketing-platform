# Open Questions & What I'd Explore Next

## Fraud & Trust
- How do we detect creator fraud at account level —
  fake followers, bot engagement — not just click anomalies?
- Should brands be able to flag creators post-campaign
  and have that feed into the matching algorithm?

## Attribution
- How do we handle multi-touch attribution when a buyer
  clicks affiliate links from multiple creators?
- Should the last-click model be the default or
  should we explore weighted attribution in v2?

## Growth
- What's the right incentive to get creators to
  activate their storefront in the first 7 days?
- Can we use campaign performance data to auto-recommend
  bid amounts to brands based on industry benchmarks?

## Monetization
- Platform fee model — flat % vs tiered by campaign volume?
- Should top-performing creators get a lower platform fee
  as a retention incentive?

## AI Matching
- At what data volume does the matching model become
  reliable enough to deprecate manual search?
- Should engagement rate be weighted higher than audience size
  for niche campaign types?

## Community
- At what usage threshold does it make sense to
  build community in-house vs continue with third-party API?
- Can community subscription data feed into
  creator discovery signals for brands?

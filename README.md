# Performance Marketing & Creator Monetization Platform

**Type:** 0→1 Platform Build
**Domain:** Creator Economy / Influencer Marketing
**Role:** Product Manager
**Status:** Shipped

## Overview
A two-sided marketplace connecting brands with content creators
for performance-based marketing campaigns.

The platform handled the full lifecycle — product catalog
management, campaign creation, creator discovery, affiliate
tracking, fraud detection, automated payouts, and creator-side
monetization through storefronts, communities, and courses.

## The Problem
Brands were running influencer campaigns manually with no
attribution or fraud protection. Creators had no unified
platform to manage deals, track earnings, or build
recurring revenue streams.

## My Role
- Defined product vision and roadmap for 0→1 build
- Mapped end-to-end flows for both brand and creator sides
- Prioritized v1 scope across 7+ feature areas
- Worked across design, engineering, and business stakeholders

## Outcomes
- Unified brand-creator workflow into one platform
- Reduced campaign setup time vs manual outreach processes
- Enabled creators to consolidate 3+ revenue streams
- Fraud detection layer reduced invalid payout exposure
